## 2. The Core Philosophy: Critical Rationalism (Popper/Deutsch)

Knowledge advances through **falsification and error correction**, not verification.

*   **P1: All observation is theory-laden.** We never observe "pure facts." We always interpret through existing theories.
*   **P2: Theories can never be proven, only disproven.** Confirming evidence doesn't prove theories true. One contradiction can prove them false.
*   **P3: Knowledge grows through error elimination.** Progress comes from eliminating falsehoods, not accumulating "truths."
*   **P4: Children are Universal Explainers.** Children's minds work like adult minds: conjecture → criticism → refinement. Coercion kills this engine.

## 3. The "Learner Without Borders" Journey

Imagine a child in an Edventures residency:

1.  **The Spark (TCS):** The child becomes genuinely interested in how local monsoons affect architecture. No adult tells them to "focus on math instead."
2.  **The Conjecture (CETR):** The child forms a theory: "Stilt houses are high because the water rises 2 meters."
3.  **The Lab (Edventures):** They use the **Maker Studio** to build a scale model and the **Offline Library** to research flood levels.
4.  **The Refutation (CETR):** They discover that the water only rises 0.5 meters. Their initial conjecture is **Refuted**.
5.  **The Search for a Better Explanation:** They interview a local builder and discover the stilts are also for ventilation and protection from animals.
6.  **The Refinement (CETR):** They **Refine** their theory into a "Hard-to-Vary" explanation.
7.  **The Village (TCS/Edventures):** They present their findings to the "Learning Village," not for a grade, but to share knowledge.

*   **Traditional School:** Coercive (No TCS) and Inductivist (No CETR).
*   **Standard Homeschool:** Often lacks the "Village" and peer-group challenge (No Edventures).

**This model is the first to achieve "Coherence": Respecting the child's mind (TCS), empowering their thinking (CETR), and providing a world worth exploring (Edventures).**
