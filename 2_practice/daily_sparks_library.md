# The Daily Spark Library: 50+ Prompts for Sovereign Learners

A "Spark" is a question that launches a **Conjecture**. These are designed to be "Why" questions that lead to causal explanations, not just "What" questions that lead to definitions.

---

### How to use these Sparks:
1.  **Pick one** during breakfast or on a walk.
2.  **Ask for a Conjecture:** "What's your boldest guess?"
3.  **Encourage Detail:** "How would that work in detail?"
4.  **Do NOT give the answer.** If they get stuck, go to the **Offline Library** together.

**Goal:** The goal isn't to get the "correct" answer by the end of the day. The goal is to practice the **Cycle of Refinement.**
